New hackerspace location

After years of corona and having a nomadic lifestyle, we finally have a location to call our own again. We have moved into what was previously "De Stadstuin" located at Europalaan 20.


Space day at the new location

That means that we're finally able to offer the perks that were available pre-corona, such as 24/7 access for members and the ability to refurnish the location according to our own wishes. The location is still labeled as "anti-kraak" and there are tentative plans for the building to be demolished in the future, but until then we effectively have a kingdom to ourselves.
